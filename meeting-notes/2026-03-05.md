## Topics Discussed

- Working group process setup, including collaboration tools (Google Doc primary, Discord backup) and biweekly cadence.
- Scope framing for governance/regulatory work, including avoiding duplication with existing industry efforts and aligning terminology.
- Proposed three-step approach: landscape analysis of existing frameworks, gap analysis for emerging agentic AI issues, and distilled guidance/principles.
- Debate on deliverable shape, including documentation-only outputs versus implementation-oriented guidance and standards.
- Inputs from members on existing frameworks and regulations (for example GDPR/PII-related controls) relevant to regulated sectors.

## Decisions

- Adopt a three-step working model: landscape analysis, gap analysis, then distilled guidance.
- Use the shared Google Doc/running notes as the primary collaboration artifact, with Discord as a secondary channel.
- Prioritize a consolidated review of current best practices/frameworks as the initial deliverable.
- Carry forward an agenda item to decide the scope boundary for implementation guidance.

## Deliverables

- Problem set statements for governance and regulatory challenges in agentic AI.
- Landscape analysis of existing standards, frameworks, regulations, and use cases.
- Gap analysis identifying where current approaches do not address emerging agentic AI risks.
- Potential distilled guidance/principles for organizations (pending scope decision).
